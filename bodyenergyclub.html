Instagram strategy for a leading vitamin, sports supplement, and smoothie bar.
overview

For this project, I developed a social media strategy and created sample content for Body Energy Club's Instagram account. I started by creating my client's profile to gather all the necessary information about the brand, such as its values, goal, target audience, and visual identity. I conducted a market research and evaluated main competitors' social media accounts to identify potential areas of improvement for Body Energy Club's content. To create sample content, I went to the Gastown location and took photos of some products.

Brand Identity
Content Strategy

After conducting an industry research, I came up with a content strategy plan for Body Energy Club's Instagram account that will aim at boosting social engagement with the brand and increase the number of followers.

  • Content Variety
  • Introduce a variety of content including photos of food, videos of stores and how smoothies are made, informational posts about health facts, nutritional advice, and recipes using products sold by Body Energy Club.
  • Get people to share your posts on their stories by posting knowledge about products, ways to be more environmentally-friendly, benefits of certain foods, inspirational workout posts, current customer incentives, promotions, and special events.
  • Get people to save Body Energy Club’s posts by adding more informational content, such as mini tutorials, health benefits of products, basically anything people want to see again.
  • Creative Visuals
  • Overall style and tone of the posts need to be consistent, recognizable at a glance. This can be achieved through the use of colour grading, fonts, and colours.
  • Introduce graphical elements in posts and stories that will grab users' attention and allow to showcase products from the website in a creative yet natural way. Mix photos, graphics and white space to reduce the overall visual load of the page.
  • In general, Body Energy Club should aim to post content that is inspiring, sparks curiosity, boosts mood. Bring the wow factor!

Conclusion
Instagram can be a powerful marketing tool for achieving business goals. It's important to have a clear understanding of your branding and voice to establish a strong online presence and stand out from the competition. This project has explored the possibilities of solidifying content aesthetic for Body Energy Club's Instagram account that will help increase brand recognition and reach larger audiences.
